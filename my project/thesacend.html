Here's some tips and advice for beginners :

1-you should start with a good warm up before exercising.
2-Try to have a snake, before workout.
3-Arrange your workout schedule so you can train your whole body by the end of the week, for example:
you workout for 6 days a week, then:
day 1: legs / glutes and hips
day2: back
day3: triceps and shoulder
day4: biceps and chest
day5: abs
day6: full body with cardio
you should know what fits you the most and schdule your training plan based on your needs, each body is diffrent from the other.
